Loan coordination for temporary exhibitions

The quiet work behind every borrowed masterpiece.

Synopia coordinates the lending of artworks between museums — requests, conditions, insurance, and transit — so a temporary show comes together without the months of back-and-forth.

What Synopia handles

Everything a loan touches, in one shared record.

01

Loan requests

Curators send and receive borrowing requests with dates, purpose, and the works in scope — no email chains to reconstruct later.

02

Condition & insurance

Condition reports and insurance valuations live alongside the loan, visible to registrars on both sides as terms evolve.

03

Availability calendar

See when a work is free to travel before you plan the show around it. Conflicts surface early, not after the catalogue is printed.

04

Transit & couriers

Coordinate crating, shipping windows, and courier handoffs, with every approval logged against a single timeline.
